Swami Vivekananda Institute of Science and Technology Admission 2023: Admission to UG courses in Engineering & Technology, Pharmacy, and Architecture streams at Swami Vivekananda Institute of Science and Technology will be done based on the WBJEE 2023. WBJEE Form Correction Window is open till January 24, 2023, for registered candidates. WBJEEB will conduct the WBJEE on April 30, 2023, and the admit card will be released on April 20, 2023. Swami Vivekananda Institute of Science and Technology - [SVIST], Kolkata, West Bengal was established in 2008 and is affiliated to Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology. The college offers undergraduate courses for B. Tech in various disciplines. The college also offers a two years master degree program for MBA and 3 years Polytechnic diploma under two specializations. Admission procedure for the following courses is done on the basis of counseling dependent upon the rank secured in West Bengal Joint Entrance Examinations WBJEE/ AIEEE. To be eligible for admission to the MBA program, the candidates must qualify for the MAT/CAT, CMAT exam. The seats are reserved for SC, ST, OBC and physically handicapped candidates in all the courses. Candidates can apply to the institute by filling the application form either online or offline.
